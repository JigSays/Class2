Student Bio

Jordan Gary

Jordan Gary hails from Miami, Florida. He attended Leewood Elmentary School, then Palmetto Middle School and graduated from Miami Killian Senior High School in 2001. He than attended Fordham University in New York City from 2001 - 2004, before joining NYC & Company. In 2007, Jordan joined Empire Force Events before starting RentChic in 2008. RentChic was a short-term furnished real estate company in Manhattan. Jordan also became a partner in SideWalk Media in 2010, which bought 'Delight Gluten-Free Magainze' in 2011 where Jordan served as Publisher. In October 2015, Delight was sold to Innovision Health Media. In 2016, Jordan began attending DePaul University working on his Master's in Computer Science.
